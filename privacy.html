Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This policy explains what My Signal collects when you use the site, the quiz and the Telegram handoff.

What we collect

  • Your quiz answers and generated preview in your browser session.
  • Basic technical data required to run the site, including language, market, device/browser information, referrer and analytics events if analytics is enabled.
  • UTM parameters and the selected plan to understand where requests come from.
  • Any information you later send voluntarily in Telegram or email.

How the information is used

  • To generate the preview and route you to the correct pricing and language experience.
  • To measure page performance and conversion events.
  • To let the manager continue the conversation with the plan, market, preview and source attached.

Storage and sharing

The landing page stores language, UTM and market data in local browser storage. If you continue into Telegram, the message content is transferred to Telegram under Telegram's own privacy terms. My Signal does not sell personal information.

Your choices

  • You can close the page at any time.
  • You can clear local browser storage from your browser settings.
  • You can contact the operator and ask to delete data shared directly in chat or by email.

Medical and emergency disclaimer

My Signal is not a medical or mental health service. It is not emergency support, diagnosis, treatment or therapy. If you need urgent help, contact local emergency or crisis support services immediately.
